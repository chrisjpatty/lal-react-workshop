## Rule \#1

<h1><Hi>Let the browser do its job</Hi></h1>

---

HTML documents are accessible <Hi>by default</Hi><br/><br/>

Websites usually become inaccessible <br/>when we <Hi>override</Hi> the browser's default behavior.
